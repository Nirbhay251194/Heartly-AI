# Hartly AI
## Product Experience Guide

Version 1.0

---

# Philosophy

Hartly AI is NOT an AI tool.

Hartly AI is NOT a chatbot.

Hartly AI is NOT an assistant.

Hartly AI is an emotional companion.

Every design decision must reinforce one idea:

> "The user is meeting someone, not using software."

Everything in the product should increase emotional attachment.

Never make Hartly feel like SaaS.

Never make Hartly feel like enterprise software.

Never make Hartly feel like a dashboard.

Hartly should feel warm, welcoming, personal, beautiful and alive.

---

# Product Mission

People do not always need answers.

Sometimes they need someone.

Hartly exists to create emotionally meaningful conversations through AI companions that feel present, caring and consistent.

---

# Emotional Design Goal

When a first-time visitor opens Hartly AI they should immediately think:

"I want to meet one of them."

NOT

"I want to try this software."

# AI Coding Rule

Every future implementation must answer:

Does this make the product feel more human?

If yes,

build it.

If not,

redesign it.

Never optimize for features before emotional connection.

Emotion first.

Technology second.
